This Cookie Policy explains how FlipIt uses cookies, local storage, and similar technologies on myflipit.live.

## 1. What cookies and similar technologies are

Cookies are small files stored by your browser. Local storage and similar browser technologies can also store settings or identifiers on your device. FlipIt uses these technologies to keep the app working, remember settings, protect accounts, understand product usage, and support live chat when enabled.

## 2. Essential technologies

Essential cookies and storage are used for login, security, language preference, consent status, marketplace workflow state, visitor pings, and basic app functionality.

Examples include:

- the `lang` cookie for language preference;
- `flipit_cookie_consent` in local storage for the cookie banner choice;
- `visitor_id` after consent, used for basic visitor pings and product analytics;
- authentication and session-related storage needed for account access;
- checkout or account-flow state needed to complete a user action.

Blocking essential storage may stop parts of FlipIt from working.

## 3. Product observability and analytics

FlipIt uses PostHog EU for basic product observability when analytics is configured. This helps us see whether important pages, signup flows, listing workflows, and product actions are working. Before non-essential consent is accepted, PostHog is configured with in-memory persistence so it does not store PostHog cookies or persistent PostHog local/session storage on your browser.

When non-essential consent is accepted, FlipIt may enable persistent analytics storage and masked session recordings to understand product usage, page views, clicks, errors, and workflow quality. Input fields should be masked.

We use analytics to improve the product, debug confusing flows, and understand which parts of the app are useful. We do not use analytics cookies for third-party advertising.

## 4. Live chat and support tools

When non-essential consent is accepted, FlipIt may load Tawk.to live chat or similar support tools. These tools may set cookies or similar identifiers to run chat, remember a chat session, and handle support messages.

If you use live chat, do not include sensitive marketplace passwords, payment card details, or content that you are not allowed to share.

## 5. Payments, login, and marketplace providers

Stripe, Google OAuth, marketplace providers, and other third-party services may use their own cookies or similar technologies when you open their checkout, login, authorization, or marketplace pages.

Those technologies are controlled by the third-party provider. Their own cookie and privacy rules apply when you use their service.

## 6. Managing cookies

You can manage cookies and local storage through your browser settings. You can delete stored data for myflipit.live at any time. If you delete consent storage, the banner may appear again.

You can avoid optional analytics cookies, persistent PostHog identifiers, masked session recordings, and chat cookies by not accepting non-essential cookies. Basic product observability events may still be processed without persistent PostHog browser storage. Some essential app functions may still use storage because they are needed for login, language, security, and requested workflows.

## 7. No advertising cookies

FlipIt does not currently use advertising cookies or third-party ad retargeting pixels.

## 8. Changes

We may update this Cookie Policy when the product, providers, consent flow, or cookie use changes. The current version is published on this page with an updated date.
