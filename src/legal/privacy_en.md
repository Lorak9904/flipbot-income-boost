This Privacy Policy explains how FlipIt handles personal data when you visit the website, create an account, connect marketplaces, prepare listings, use AI-assisted features, contact support, or pay for a plan.

## 1. Controller and contact

The controller for personal data processed through FlipIt is the FlipIt operator. You can contact us at myflipit@arrpo.com.

Use this email for privacy questions, account deletion, access requests, correction requests, objection requests, or other data-protection matters.

## 2. Data we process

Depending on how you use FlipIt, we may process:

- account data, such as email address, name, login provider, language preference, and account settings;
- authentication and security data, such as tokens, session identifiers, IP address, device/browser data, and login events;
- marketplace connection data, such as OAuth tokens, marketplace account identifiers, session cookies you provide, country/account settings, and connection status;
- listing data, such as item titles, descriptions, categories, attributes, prices, photos, image URLs, marketplace drafts, import data, publishing status, and error messages;
- billing data, such as selected plan, credits, checkout status, payment provider references, invoice details, tax or currency data, and support records;
- support and communication data, such as email messages, forms, feedback, and live chat messages;
- analytics data, such as page views, clicks, UI events, approximate technical data, and masked session recordings when analytics is enabled;
- cookie and local storage data, described in the Cookie Policy.

## 3. Why we process data

We process data to:

- create and secure user accounts;
- provide marketplace listing, import, draft, review, and publishing workflows;
- connect and maintain marketplace accounts;
- generate AI-assisted listing drafts, categories, descriptions, and other listing fields;
- store listing history, status, errors, and user settings;
- process subscriptions, credits, invoices, taxes, fraud checks, and payment support;
- answer support requests and improve the product;
- detect abuse, debug errors, maintain security, and protect the service;
- meet legal, accounting, and compliance duties.

## 4. Legal bases

For users in the European Economic Area, we rely on the legal bases that match the purpose:

- contract performance when we provide the FlipIt account, marketplace tools, listing workflows, support, and billing features;
- legitimate interests when we keep the service secure, debug product issues, prevent abuse, improve workflows, and maintain business records;
- consent when consent is required for non-essential cookies, analytics, live chat tools, or optional communications;
- legal obligation when we must keep records, respond to lawful requests, handle tax/accounting duties, or comply with applicable law.

## 5. AI-assisted processing

FlipIt may send listing-related content, such as photos, image URLs, item details, titles, descriptions, category context, and marketplace fields, to AI providers to generate or improve listing drafts.

Do not upload content that you are not allowed to process or send to a third-party provider. Always review AI output before publishing. AI output may be inaccurate, incomplete, or unsuitable for a specific marketplace.

## 6. Service providers and recipients

We may share data with providers that help operate FlipIt, including:

- hosting and infrastructure providers, including DigitalOcean and Cloudflare/R2;
- AI providers, including OpenAI, for listing analysis and draft generation;
- analytics providers, including PostHog EU, if analytics is enabled;
- payment providers, including Stripe, for checkout, billing, invoices, fraud prevention, and tax handling;
- authentication providers, including Google OAuth, if you use Google login;
- live chat/support providers, including Tawk.to, if the chat tool is loaded or used;
- marketplace providers, such as OLX, Vinted, Facebook Marketplace, eBay, Allegro, and Etsy, when you connect accounts, import listings, or publish through supported workflows;
- email and support tools used to send account, security, support, or service messages.

Providers process data only as needed for their role, under their own terms where they are independent providers or as processors where applicable.

## 7. International transfers

Some providers may process data outside the European Economic Area. Where required, transfers should rely on appropriate safeguards, such as adequacy decisions, standard contractual clauses, provider transfer terms, or other lawful transfer mechanisms.

## 8. Retention

We keep data for as long as needed to provide FlipIt, maintain account history, meet legal or accounting duties, resolve disputes, prevent abuse, and improve security.

Account and listing data is kept while the account is active. Some records may remain after account deletion when retention is required for billing, fraud prevention, security logs, legal claims, or compliance. Marketplace tokens and session data should be removed when an integration is disconnected or when they are no longer needed.

## 9. Your rights

Depending on the law that applies to you, you may have the right to access your data, correct it, delete it, restrict processing, object to processing, receive a copy of your data, or withdraw consent.

To use these rights, contact myflipit@arrpo.com. We may need to verify your identity before acting on a request.

If you are in Poland or the European Union, you may also complain to a data protection authority. In Poland, this is the President of the Personal Data Protection Office (UODO).

## 10. Security

We use HTTPS and technical protections intended to protect account, marketplace, listing, and billing data. Marketplace tokens and sensitive connection material should be protected on the backend.

No online service is perfectly secure. Keep your account, email, marketplace accounts, devices, and browser sessions protected.

## 11. Children

FlipIt is intended for people who can lawfully run marketplace selling activity and enter into these Terms. The service is not intended for children.

## 12. Cookies and analytics

FlipIt uses cookies, local storage, and similar technologies for login, language, consent, security, visitor pings, product analytics, and live chat. The Cookie Policy explains these technologies in more detail.

## 13. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy when the product, providers, law, or data practices change. The current version is published on this page with an updated date.
